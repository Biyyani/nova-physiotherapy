Meet Our Physiotherapist

Dr. Chenna Daniel

Consultant Physiotherapist
As an experienced physiotherapist, Dr. Daniel holds a B.P.T. degree from the prestigious Apollo Institute of Medical Sciences in Hyderabad. He is a Certified Mulligan Practitioner and an active member of the Indian Association of Physiotherapists, staying up-to-date on the latest industry advancements. Dr. Daniel specializes in manual therapy techniques, taping techniques, cupping therapy, and dry needling techniques, providing effective treatment for various conditions. With over five years of experience, he offers personalized, patient-centered care to help individuals find relief from pain, improve mobility, and achieve their physical goals. Dr. Daniel is committed to providing the highest standard of care and helping his patients achieve optimal health

What is Physiotherapy and why do you need it?

Physiotherapy, also known as physical therapy, is a healthcare profession that involves the use of physical methods such as exercise, manual therapy, and electrotherapy to improve the function and mobility of the body.

How Physiotherapy helps in Back Pain

Back pain is a common condition that affects millions of people worldwide. While there are many different causes of back pain, it is often caused by muscle strain or injury, poor posture, or degenerative conditions such as arthritis.

How Physiotherapy helps in Elbow Pain

Elbow pain is a common condition that can occur due to various reasons, such as overuse, injury, or degenerative conditions such as arthritis. The elbow joint is critical for many daily activities, including lifting, gripping, and reaching. Therefore, elbow pain can significantly impact an individual's quality of life and limit their ability to perform routine tasks.
